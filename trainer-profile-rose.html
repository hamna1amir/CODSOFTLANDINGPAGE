Rosy Rivera

Yoga Instructor

About Rosy

Rosy Rivera is a certified yoga instructor with a passion for holistic wellness. With her expertise in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Yin yoga styles, she guides her students on a journey of physical and mental transformation.

Classes Offered

Rosy offers a variety of classes including Hatha for strength and balance, Vinyasa for flow and flexibility, and Yin for deep relaxation and meditation. Her classes are suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.
